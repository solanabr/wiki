# About Superteam Brazil

Superteam Brazil is the official Solana community hub in Brazil, connecting developers, founders, creators, and investors building Web3's future. Founded in 2024, we are part of the global Superteam network — powering the next wave of Solana builders in Latin America's largest economy.

With 60+ active members, 247+ builders, $3M+ in capital facilitated, and 40+ ecosystem partners, we serve as the bridge between global Solana projects and the Brazilian market.
