# Our Mission

The Solana hackathon theme is "Frontier," aimed at exploring new territories within the Solana ecosystem. The initiative strengthens **[Superteam Brasil](https://www.superteam.com.br/pt)**, "a community with the best builders in web3 helping Solana grow worldwide."

## Three Objectives

**1. Developer Support**
Provide expertise and resources to help participants find teammates, validate concepts, and build projects in the Solana ecosystem.

**2. Brazilian Excellence**
Metrics: participation, submitted projects, project caliber, and social media engagement.

**3. Team Building**
Winners and significant contributors join Superteam Brasil in marketing, sales, product, and development roles.

> Brazil's performance influences future funding and resources for Solana's growth in the region.
