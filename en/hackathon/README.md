# Hackathon

Welcome to the participant guide for the Solana global hackathon, presented by Colosseum! This guide provides all the information you need to participate, compete, and succeed in the world's largest web3 hackathon.

## Frontier — Solana Global Hackathon 2026

- **Period**: April 6 to May 11, 2026
- **Format**: Online + in-person events in 20+ countries
- **Registration**: [arena.colosseum.org](https://arena.colosseum.org?ref=brasil)
- **Official information**: [colosseum.com/frontier](https://colosseum.com/frontier)
- **Brazil Site**: [hackathonsolana.com](https://hackathonsolana.com/)
- **Brazil Track**: [superteam.fun/earn/listing/hackathon-superteambrasil](https://superteam.fun/earn/listing/hackathon-superteambrasil)

## Prizes (Summary)

- **Global Hackathon**: $260,000 (1x $50k Grand Champion, 20x $10k Track Winners, 1x $5k University, 1x $5k Open Source)
- **Colosseum Accelerator**: up to 10 teams receive $250,000 each in pre-seed capital
- **Brazil Track**: $10,000 distributed among the top 10 Brazilian teams
